# State of Promises Report

This MDX page is a long-form reporting template for quarterly accountability notes.

## Editorial Standard

Claims are not marked completed without documentary evidence. A manifesto line, press statement, or public rally quote is
stored as a claim until it is matched with a government resolution, budget release, department dashboard, audit report,
tender record, court filing, or verified field evidence.

## Suggested Quarterly Structure

1. Promise completion overview.
2. Budget implementation gap.
3. District-level development changes.
4. Project delays and cost escalation.
5. Evidence added since the previous update.
6. Corrections and moderation log.
